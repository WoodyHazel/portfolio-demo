My Work

I am a web developer. I am a web designer as well since the two go hand in hand, however I am a web developer first. That's because I love coding. I love learning new things and improving on the things I know.

I am a "self-taught" web developer. I learned to code through various online platforms such as Udemy, FreeCodeCamp, and Youtube.

About Website

This is MY website.

It is a space for me to showcase my skills as a web developer and designer.

Everything you see and use, or in other words the entire User Experience and User Interface, was designed and coded by me. The ONLY external resources that are utilized are Font Awesome's library for icons, Google Fonts for fonts, and several websites for stock images and music. There are no frameworks or libraries for the HTML, CSS, or JavaScript.

All the code I wrote from scratch, but of course I had help along the way in the form of online courses and tutorials.
